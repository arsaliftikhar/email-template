LIQ Team Tokens Burned

We are excited to announce our biggest LIQ token burn so far! We will burn 16% of the total token supply, which is 100% of the original team tokens. The total supply of Liquidus will be reduced from 93 million to 77 million LIQ.

 

Our mission is long term and we would like to show that in the most meaningful way we thought was possible, hence
the burn. 
 

To further signal our long term commitment and build trust, we are starting the clock from 0. All team members will have to earn their tokens from today onwards, through value delivered to the project over time. 

 

The first burn will happen on 15th October at 8pm UTC.
